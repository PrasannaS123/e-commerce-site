Walmart E-commerce Registration
An e-commerce registration form is used by e-commerce businesses to register their goods or services that are available for sale.
Personal Information
Email is required. Please enter a valid email address.
Username is required. Username must be at least 5 characters long.
Password is required. Password must be at least 8 characters long. Password must contain at least one lowercase letter, one uppercase letter, one digit, and one special character.
Phone Number is required. Please enter a valid 10-digit phone number.
Address is required.
State is required.
Pin is required. Please enter a 6-digit Pin code.
